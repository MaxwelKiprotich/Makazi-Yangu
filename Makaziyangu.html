Step 1

SEARCH FOR A HOME

  • Go to the homepage
  • Use the search bar to select:
    1. Location (e.g., Kilimani, Rongai)
    2. Property type (For Sale / For Rent)
    3. Price range and other filters
  • Click on "Find Home" to explore listings

Step 2

REVIEW SUGGESTED HOUSES

  • Browse through the list of matching properties.
  • Click on any listing to view:
    1. Photos
    2. Price
    3. Features
    4. Agent/owner details
  • Save your favorites by clicking the heart icon.

Step 3

CONTACT AGENT OR LANDLORD

  • Use the contact form, phone number, or "Book Viewing" button on the listing page.
  • Schedule a visit or ask questions directly.
  • You'll receive a notification or email with agent response.
