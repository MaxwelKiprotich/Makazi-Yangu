FAQs

Got questions? We have answers to help you make informed property decisions


Is there a fee to list my property?

Yes, we offer both free and premium property listing options. Premium listings get higher visibility and additional marketing features.

How do I create an account and what benefits does it offer?

Creating an account is simple and free. With an account, you can save your favorite properties, receive personalized alerts for new listings, and track your inquiries or appointments - all in one place.

How can I list my property on Makazi Yangu?

Listing your property is straightforward. Sign up or log in, click on “List Your Property” and fill out the required details. Upload high-quality photos and a detailed description. Our team will review your submission to ensure a smooth, accurate listing process.


How do I schedule a property viewing?

Simply click on a listing you like and select “Schedule Viewing.” Our team will coordinate with the seller or agent to set up a convenient time.


Is Makazi Yangu safe for transactions?

Absolutely! We verify all property listings and ensure that transactions are secure and transparent.


Is my personal data secure on Makazi Yangu?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. Your personal information is kept confidential and never shared with third parties without your consent. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
