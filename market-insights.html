Kenya Real Estate Market Overview - April 2025

Current Market Trends

The Kenyan property market is seeing a steady increase in demand for affordable housing in urban areas, especially in Nairobi and Mombasa. Developers are shifting towards gated communities and mixed-use developments to meet the growing needs of middle-income earners.

Property Value Statistics

+6.2% Property Value Growth
KES 9.5M Avg. Urban Home
KES 45K Avg. Monthly Rent
12% ROI in Suburban Areas

Insights by Region

Nairobi: Westlands, Kilimani, and Ruaka continue to show high rental yields.
Mombasa: Tourist areas like Nyali are seeing increased interest in short-term rentals.
Kisumu: Expansion of road infrastructure is boosting peripheral land values.
Nakuru: An emerging urban area with lots of opportunities.
